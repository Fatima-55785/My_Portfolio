Introduction of Computer

The computer is an electronic device that has revolutionized the way we live, work, and communicate.
It has become an essential tool in modern life.

History of Computer

The first computer was invented in the 19th century by Charles Babbage.

Components of Computer

A computer consists of several key components, including:

  1. Memory (RAM)
  2. Storage Device (Hard Drive)
  3. Input Devices (Keyboard, Mouse)
  4. Output Devices (Monitor, Printer)

Uses of Computer

Computer have the wide range of uses, including:

  • Communication (email, social media)
  • Education (online learning, research)
  • Entertainment (games, movies, music)
  • Business (word processing, spreadsheets)
  • Healthcare (medical records, diagnosis)
